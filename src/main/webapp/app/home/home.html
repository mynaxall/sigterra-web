Signature marketing reinvented

Reinvent the email signature and turn it into a sophisticated marketing machine, guaranteed to turn your email traffic into engaged leads.​

Sigterra is the only signature marketing platform that lets you add a lot information to your email signature – about your company, products, customer testimonials, even your awards and hobbies, all in a simple business card format.​
