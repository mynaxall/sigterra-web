Decreasing Attention Span

B2B Marketers care about capturing and keeping their prospects’ attention. A recent study from Microsoft Cananda tells us that attention span for individuals have decreased from 12 sec in 2000 to 8 sec in 2016. This makes the job of a marketer incredibly difficult.

To make matters worse, companies have developed too much content over the years to help with SEO and inbound traffic. Prospects are increasingly getting lost in this content forest.

Sigterra’s narrative automation platform not only helps you tell a quick bite-sized story to your prospects to ensure that you make the best of the attention you receive.
